How to Play

1. Move the Tiles

Click on any tile adjacent to the empty space to move it.

2. Arrange Numbers

Arrange the numbers in ascending order from left to right, top to bottom.

3. Win the Game

Complete the puzzle with the fewest moves possible to achieve the highest score.

Game Features

Simple Controls

Easy-to-use click interface that works on both desktop and mobile devices.

Move Counter

Track your progress with the move counter and try to beat your best score.

Instant Reset

Start a new game at any time with the reset button.
