How to Play

1. Game Objective

Arrange the numbers in ascending order, with the empty space at the bottom right.

2. Moving Tiles

Click or tap any number adjacent to the empty space to slide it into the empty position.

3. Winning the Game

Complete the puzzle by arranging all numbers in order. Try to achieve the best score!

Game Features

Multiple Sizes

Choose from 3x3, 4x4, or 5x5 grid sizes for different challenges.

Track Progress

Monitor your moves, time, and best scores as you improve.
